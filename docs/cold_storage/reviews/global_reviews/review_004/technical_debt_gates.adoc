== Bottom line for sequencing

ODS is a *gate*, not a *blocker*. The two product directions with live
current/major findings -- the FK-helper unresolved-value contract and the
minimal meta-boundary contract -- do not stress ODS parser/render/readback and
may proceed without touching it. Only when a slice introduces a *new visible
carrier structure* does the ODS preparation become a prerequisite.
