=== Summary

You have now seen the three ways to use *Spreadsheet-Handling*:

1. **CLI shims** - legacy example commands such as `sheets-pack` and `sheets-unpack`, while `sheets-run` remains the maintained primary entry point.
2. **Ad-hoc steps files** - your own YAML-defined pipelines for quick experiments.
3. **Integrated configs** - full project setups with named pipelines and profiles.

All three modes rely on the same underlying engine,
so you can start simple and evolve your setup as your needs grow.

The next chapters explain the command-line options in detail,
describe the configuration format,
and list the available steps you can combine into your own workflows.
