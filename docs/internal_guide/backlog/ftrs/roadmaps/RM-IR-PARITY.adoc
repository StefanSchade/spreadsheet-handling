===== RM-IR-PARITY: Parity between IR renderer and legacy renderer

We are refactoring the spreadsheet-facing backends, which were initially called directly by the domain layer, to route through an intermediate representation (IR).
The roadmap defines progressive slices toward functional parity, CI-proven stability, and final deprecation of the legacy OpenPyXL path.

* *P1 — IR Parity Slice v1 (current)*
- *Feature:* <<FTR-IR-WRITEPATH-P1 — IR write pipeline with openpyxl renderer: “Parity Slice v1” (P1) and wire CI around it,FTR-IR-WRITEPATH-P1>>
- *Scope:* Header style + AutoFilter + optional freeze row, simple in-list validations, sheet names/order, minimal meta sheet.
- *Outcome:* Small parity suite runs in CI under `SH_XLSX_BACKEND=ir`; legacy renderer remains default elsewhere.

* *P2 — IR Parity Slice v2: Multi-headers & merges*
- *Feature:* <<FTR-MULTIHEADER-P2 — IR Renderer “Parity Slice v2” (P2) Multi-row/column headers (with merges) + Optional Flattening at I/O,FTR-MULTIHEADER-P2>>
- *Scope:* 2D header blocks, horizontal/vertical merges, optional flattening during read/write (configurable), parity fixtures for typical 2–3 level headers.

* *P3 — IR Parity Slice v3: Parity Verification & Temporary CI Safeguard*
- *Feature:* <<FTR-PARITY-IR-P3 — IR Renderer Parity with Legacy OpenPyXL,FTR-PARITY-IR-P3>>
- *Scope:*
** Establish a short-lived CI job (`parity-ir`) that compares IR vs. legacy outputs on a fixed fixture set.
** Triggered only on relevant paths or nightly; runs in pinned, reproducible environment.
** Ensures parity in layout, style, validations, and metadata persistence.
** Stability gate (e.g., 10 consecutive green runs across matrix) unlocks IR default activation.
** No product-surface flags — safeguard limited to CI/testing only.
- *Outcome:* Verified confidence that IR renderer behavior matches legacy across representative workbooks; IR path ready to become default.

* *P4 — IR Parity Slice v4: Cleanup and Forward-Only Migration*
- *Feature:* <<FTR-CLEANUP-IR-P4 — Remove Legacy Renderer and Parity Safeguard,FTR-CLEANUP-IR-P4>>
- *Scope:*
** Remove the legacy OpenPyXL renderer and all associated flags, code paths, and Make targets.
** Delete the temporary `parity-ir` CI job and convert fixtures to IR-only golden snapshot tests.
** Finalize IR as the sole backend; close out `docs/legacy_gap.md`.
** Release notes mark removal of the legacy path and completion of IR migration.
- *Outcome:* Clean, single-path IR renderer implementation with no fallback or CI safeguard remaining.
The system runs fully IR-backed, with reduced maintenance surface and simplified release pipeline.

-> Confidence to deprecate the legacy path and complete the IR migration.

NOTE: A short-lived parity CI job is best practice for compiler/renderer migrations when refactors and dependency updates are still in motion. Use **only** during active migration; plan removal immediately after the stability gate.
